Introducing Roomies

For organizing all your housemates needs in one easy place

Get Started

Features

  • Easy to Use

    Have each roommate create an account and then sign in to your house to begin using Roomies! Add needed items to the grocery list and all your house tasks to the chore list.

  • Simple & Minimal

    Utilize the clean dashboard design to stay organized and up to date on house groceries still needed to be bought and monthly, weekly, or even daily chores to be done.

  • Roommate Satisfaction

    Roomies is a convenient method of ensuring proper communication and completion of your shared housing needs for every kind of roommate. No excuses for an empty fridge or a messy home!

Dashboard

The dashboard interface, seen in the image below, is the main method of visual communication between roommates. Each roommate in the house can see both the Grocery List and the Chore List view in their dashbaord. Additionally, roommates can add items and tasks to both lists or mark them as bought or complete to notify their other roomies of the change. Groceries and chores can be removed or edited as needed. The dashboard allows for the a simple and easy to use experience for Roomies users.

Get Started

Are you ready to start using Roomies with your housemates? Great! The process is as easy as having each roommate create an account and then sign in to your house to begin using Roomies! Add needed items to be bought to the grocery list and all of your montly, weekly, or even daily household tasks to the chore list.
